BIO

I am passionate about my career as a technical writer, and as an educator. My strengths include writing focused, task-oriented and user-oriented documentation; developing and implementing in-class and eLearning curricula; and research to ensure that both my writing and instructional materials are accurate, complete, and usable. On a personal note: I am a Part-Time Instructor of Communication at KSU, as well as a KSU Communication alum. I have more than 10 years of experience in media, ranging from editorial and reporting, to print advertising, to technical writing. In addition to working as a part-time instructor, I am also a technical writer for a software company. During my free time, I enjoy reading, cooking, and spending time with my family. A little-known fact about me: I love retro video games, and have an original NES (Nintendo Entertainment System) circa 1983. My two favorite games are Super Mario Brothers and Tetris.
